About Us

Shyam Distributor began its journey 40 years ago as a pharmaceutical wholesale business in Jaipur. Over time, our commitment to reliability and service excellence led us to evolve into a full-scale Carry and Forwarding (C&F) and warehousing partner.

For the past 15 years, we've been the official C&F agent for over 20 leading pharmaceutical and healthcare companies, enabling seamless supply chain operations across Rajasthan.

With a centrally located warehouse, dedicated team, and strong logistics coordination, we ensure regulatory compliance, stock transparency, and timely deliveries - making us the trusted distribution hub for manufacturers looking to scale in the region.
