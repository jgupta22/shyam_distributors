What is a C&F (Carrying & Forwarding) agent and how does it help my business?
A C&F agent manages storage, handling, and distribution of your products within a region. This includes receiving goods, maintaining inventory, processing orders, and dispatching to distributors or retailers.

It helps reduce logistics complexity, improve delivery timelines, and scale distribution without setting up your own infrastructure.
